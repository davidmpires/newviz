# SEO Copy-Paste Guide for portnwine.com Blog

**How to use this document:**
For each blog post, go to your Squarespace dashboard:
1. Pages > Blog > hover over the post > click the gear icon (Settings)
2. Go to the **SEO** tab
3. Paste the **SEO Title** into the "SEO Title" field
4. Paste the **SEO Description** into the "SEO Description" field
5. Click **Save**

For URL slug changes: In the same Settings panel, go to the **General** tab and update the "URL Slug" field. **Important:** Squarespace will auto-create a 301 redirect when you change a slug.
